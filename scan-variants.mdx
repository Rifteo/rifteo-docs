</Note>

## Scan Variants

ASM comes in three depths, matched to your needs by the Rifteo team:

| Variant | Depth | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **ASM Soft** | Lightweight scan, fastest turnaround | Quick, frequent checks with minimal noise |
| **ASM Medium** | Balanced depth and speed | Regular monitoring for most clients |
| **ASM Full** | Deepest scan, most thorough discovery | Periodic in-depth reviews, or your first ASM scan |

<Note>
A deeper variant takes longer to complete but discovers more. You'll see which variant was used for each scan in your scan history.
</Note>
